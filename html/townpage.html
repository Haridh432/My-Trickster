TOWN

El Quixote

 Bathed in the golden glow of perpetual sunlight, El Quixote is a haven for peaceful creatures and bustling towns. Fields of radiant flowers sway in the gentle breeze, and vibrant butterflies dance through the air. Here, players can embark on quests, trade goods, and bask in the serenity of nature.

Humpty, City of Wisdom

 Nestled within the ancient Enchanted Grove, Humpty is an ethereal city suspended among the towering trees. Home to the wise and mystical elves, Humpty is a hub of arcane knowledge and magic. The city's luminescent crystals illuminate the night, creating a captivating display that enchants all who gaze upon it.

Stallion

 Standing tall against the horizon, Stallion is a gathering place for like-minded players to form alliances and embark on quests together. Guild banners flutter in the breeze as guild members strategize and plan their next move against the encroaching Shadowbane.
